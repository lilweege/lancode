Instructions

  1. Pick a question from the list that you think would be a suitable challenge for you.
    • If you read a question and it's too easy or too difficult, pick another one. Try to aim for 10-20 minutes per question.
    • If you find a question that you've already solved, please skip it.
  2. Enter your name (or an anonymous alias) in the Claimed by field and click submit; this lets others know not to interfere with you.
  3. Solve the question in LeetCode.
  4. Copy your accepted submission link, paste is in the Submission link box, and click submit.
